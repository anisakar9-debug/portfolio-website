01   About

Science-trained. Strategy-driven.
Berlin-based.

I am a founder and business strategist with over seven years of experience spanning mobility, life sciences, AI and biotech. My career bridges scientific rigour with commercial instinct — built to operate where complexity and opportunity intersect.

I lead growth through both inbound and outbound marketing strategies, combining content and brand-led attraction with direct pipeline development and partnership channels. My approach to fundraising is relationship-first, grounded in investor psychology and long-term trust building.

I founded Belleza Biotech, directed strategic operations at NISER, contributed to transformation at DB S-Bahn Berlin and advised Bayer AG on ESG frameworks. Every engagement sharpens my ability to move fast, communicate with precision and deliver outcomes that endure.
